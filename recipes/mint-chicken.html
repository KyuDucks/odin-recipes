Mint Chicken

Description

Chicken that is cooked with lemon juice and mint leaves. It brings a nice balance of acidity and minty freshness to a meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless boneless chicken breast halves
  • clove of garlic
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint leaves

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mix the chicken and garlic
  2. Toss with flour to coat. shake off the excess
  3. heat margarine in a large skillet ove medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned
  4. Add the mint leaves and lemon juice. Cover and steam until chicken is cooked through.
